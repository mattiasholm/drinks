# Amaretto Sour

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Disaronno`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 3 cl `simple syrup`
- 2 cl `egg white`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 2 dashes of `Angostura Bitter`

## Instructions
- Add Disaronno
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Add ice and dry shake for 10 seconds, then shake for another 10 seconds
- Add 2 dashes of Angostura Bitter

## Served in:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- 5/5
