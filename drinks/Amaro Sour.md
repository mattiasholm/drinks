# Amaro Sour

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `amaro`
- 2 cl `bourbon`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 1 `egg white`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add amaro
- Add bourbon
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
