# Angostura Colada

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 cl `dark rum`
- 6 cl `cream of coconut`
- 10 cl `pineapple juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add dark rum
- Add cream of coconut
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Hurricane glass
