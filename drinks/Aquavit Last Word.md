# Aquavit Last Word

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `aquavit`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `maraschino liqueur`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 `maraschino cherry`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add aquavit
- Add Chartreuse
- Add maraschino liqueur
- Add lime juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add maraschino cherry

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
