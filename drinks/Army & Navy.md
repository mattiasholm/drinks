# Army & Navy

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `gin`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `orgeat`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add gin
- Add lemon juice
- Add orgeat
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
