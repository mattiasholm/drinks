# Attention

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `dry vermouth`
- 0.5 cl `violet liqueur`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Orange Bitters`
- 0.5 cl `absinthe`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rinse the glass with absinthe
- Add Angostura Orange Bitters
- Add gin
- Add dry vermouth
- Add violet liqueur
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add lemon peel

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
