# Baltimore Bang

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `apricot brandy`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add bourbon
- Add apricot brandy
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
