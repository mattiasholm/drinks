# Banana Boulevardier

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `bourbon`
- 1 cl `Campari`
- 1 cl `banana liqueur`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions
- Add clear ice cube
- Add bourbon
- Add Campari
- Add banana liqueur
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
