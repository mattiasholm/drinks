# Blue Hawaii

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `blue curaçao`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 cl `pineapple juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add blue curaçao
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Hurricane glass
