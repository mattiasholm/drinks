# Blue Ocean

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `Blue Curaçao`
- 1 cl `lemon juice`
- 10 cl `7 Up`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add Blue Curaçao
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice and stir
- Fill up with 7 Up
- Garnish with lemon wedges

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- 3/5
