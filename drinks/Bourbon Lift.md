# Bourbon Lift

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `coffee liqueur`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 2 cl `orgeat`
- 5 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add bourbon
- Add coffee liqueur
- Add cream
- Add orgeat
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add soda water

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
