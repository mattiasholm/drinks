# Brandy Eggnog

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `cognac`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 1 `egg`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add cognac
- Add cream
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
