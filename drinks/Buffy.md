# Buffy

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `Passoã`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 cl `orange juice`
- 2 cl `passion fruit juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add dark rum
- Add Passoã
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add orange juice
- Add passion fruit juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Hurricane glass
