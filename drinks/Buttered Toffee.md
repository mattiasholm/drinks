# Buttered Toffee

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `butterscotch liqueur`
- 2 cl `Baileys`
- 1 cl `amaretto`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add butterscotch liqueur
- Add Baileys
- Add amaretto
- Add cream
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
