# Caipirinha

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `cachaça`
- 1 pcs `lime`
- 2 tbsp `cane sugar`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Cut off the ends of the lime and then cut it in half
- Cut away the white pulp in the middle of the lime
- Cut each half in 4 pieces 
- Add the sugar
- Muddle the lime pieces to extract the juice and dissolve the sugar
- Add crushed ice
- Add cachaça

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- 3/5
