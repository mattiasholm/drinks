# Cinnamon Bun

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `Fireball`
- 2 cl `Licor 43`
- 8 cl `milk`
- 1 ml `cardamom`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 2 dashes of `cinnamon`

## Instructions:
- Add Fireball
- Add Licor 43
- Add milk
- 
- Add cardamom
- Add ice and dry shake for 10 seconds, then shake for another 10 seconds
- Garnish with cinnamon

## Served in:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- 2/5
