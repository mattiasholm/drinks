# Corpse Reviver

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `dry vermouth`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 0.5 cl `absinthe`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rinse the glass with absinthe
- Add gin
- Add triple sec
- Add dry vermouth
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
