# Division Bell

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `mezcal`
- 2 cl `Aperol`
- 1 cl `maraschino liqueur`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add mezcal
- Add Aperol
- Add maraschino liqueur
- Add lime juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
