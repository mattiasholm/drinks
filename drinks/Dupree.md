# Dupree

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `elderflower liqueur`
- 1 cl `Aperol`
- 0.5 cl `absinthe`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rinse the glass with absinthe
- Add gin
- Add elderflower liqueur
- Add Aperol
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
