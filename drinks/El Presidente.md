# El Presidente

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `dry vermouth`
- 1 cl `triple sec`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add dry vermouth
- Add triple sec
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add lemon peel

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
