# Elder Fashioned

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 1 cl `elderflower liqueur`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Orange Bitters`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions
- Add clear ice cube
- Add Angostura Orange Bitters
- Add bourbon
- Add elderflower liqueur
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
