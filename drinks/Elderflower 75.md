# Elderflower 75

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `elderflower liqueur`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 5 cl `sparkling wine`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add elderflower liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add sparkling wine

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
