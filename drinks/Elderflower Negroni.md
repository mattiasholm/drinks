# Elderflower Negroni

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `Campari`
- 1 cl `elderflower liqueur`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions
- Add clear ice cube
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add elderflower liqueur
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
