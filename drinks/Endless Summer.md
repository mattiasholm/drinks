# Endless Summer

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Campari`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 2 cl `pineapple juice`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
