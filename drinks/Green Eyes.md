# Green Eyes

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 1 `egg white`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Chartreuse
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
