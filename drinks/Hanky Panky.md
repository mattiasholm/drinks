# Hanky Panky

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 0.5 cl `Fernet-Branca`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add Fernet-Branca
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
