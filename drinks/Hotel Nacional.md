# Hotel Nacional

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `apricot brandy`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 2 cl `pineapple juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add apricot brandy
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass

## Rating
- ★★★★★
