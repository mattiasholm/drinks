# Hurricane

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `grenadine`
- 4 cl `orange juice`
- 6 cl `passion fruit juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add grenadine
- Add orange juice
- Add passion fruit juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Hurricane glass

- ★★★★★
