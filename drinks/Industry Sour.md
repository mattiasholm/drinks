# Industry Sour

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `Fernet-Branca`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Fernet-Branca
- Add Chartreuse
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
