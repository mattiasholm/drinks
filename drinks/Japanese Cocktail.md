# Japanese Cocktail

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `cognac`
- 1 cl `orgeat`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add cognac
- Add orgeat
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add lemon peel

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
