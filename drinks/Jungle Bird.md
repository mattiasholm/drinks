# Jungle Bird

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `Campari`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 4 cl `pineapple juice`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add dark rum
- Add Campari
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
