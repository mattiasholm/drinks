# Last Flight

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `Aperol`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add bourbon
- Add Chartreuse
- Add Aperol
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
