# Lauwiliwilinukunuku'oi'oi

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `pineapple juice`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `orgeat`
- 2 dashes `Peychaud's Bitters`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Peychaud's Bitters
- Add dark rum
- Add pineapple juice
- Add lemon juice
- Add orgeat
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Highball glass
