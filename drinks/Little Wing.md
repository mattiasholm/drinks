# Little Wing

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `elderflower liqueur`
- 2 cl `Aperol`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 5 `cucumber slices`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Muddle cucumber slices
- Add bourbon
- Add elderflower liqueur
- Add Aperol
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
