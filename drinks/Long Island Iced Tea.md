# Long Island Iced Tea

## Ingredients
- 1 cl `vodka`
- 1 cl `tequila blanco`
- 1 cl `white rum`
- 1 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 cl `Coca-Cola`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add vodka
- Add tequila blanco
- Add white rum
- Add gin
- Add triple sec
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add Coca-Cola
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass
