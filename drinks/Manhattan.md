# Manhattan

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `rye whiskey`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 `maraschino cherry`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add rye whiskey
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add maraschino cherry

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
