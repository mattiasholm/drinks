# Mary Pickford

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 1 cl `maraschino liqueur`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 4 cl `pineapple juice`
- 1 `maraschino cherry`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add maraschino liqueur
- Add lime juice
- Add grenadine
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add maraschino cherry

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
