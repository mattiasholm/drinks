# Naked & Famous

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `mezcal`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `Aperol`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add mezcal
- Add Chartreuse
- Add Aperol
- Add lime juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
