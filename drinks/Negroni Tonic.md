# Negroni Tonic

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Campari`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add tonic water
- Add ice cubes
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Copa glass
