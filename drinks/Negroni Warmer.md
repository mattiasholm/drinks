# Negroni Warmer

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Campari`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 6 cl `hot water`
- 2 tsp `white sugar`
- 5 cl `whipped cream`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add hot water
- Add white sugar
- Add whipped cream

## Glassware
- Irish Coffee glass

- ★★★★★
