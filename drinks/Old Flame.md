# Old Flame

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `Campari`
- 1 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 2 cl `orange juice`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add triple sec
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add orange juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
