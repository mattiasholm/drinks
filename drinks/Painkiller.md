# Painkiller

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 6 cl `cream of coconut`
- 2 cl `orange juice`
- 8 cl `pineapple juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add dark rum
- Add cream of coconut
- Add orange juice
- Add pineapple juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Hurricane glass
