# Queen's Park Swizzle

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `demerara syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 10 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add demerara syrup
- Muddle mint leaves
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Highball glass
