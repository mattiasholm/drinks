# Red Raider

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add bourbon
- Add triple sec
- Add lemon juice
- Add grenadine
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
