# Rum Paloma

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 5 cl `grapefruit juice`
- 5 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add grapefruit juice
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass

- ★★★★☆
