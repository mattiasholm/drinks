# Scarlet O'Hara

## Ingredients:
- 6 cl `Southern Comfort`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 10 cl `cranberry juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Southern Comfort
- Add lime juice
- Add ice and stir
- Fill up with cranberry juice
- Garnish with lime wedges

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- 5/5
