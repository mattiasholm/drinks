# Scorpion

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `cognac`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `orgeat`
- 10 cl `orange juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add cognac
- Add lemon juice
- Add orgeat
- Add orange juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Hurricane glass
