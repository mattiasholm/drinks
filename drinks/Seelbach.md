# Seelbach

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `bourbon`
- 1 cl `triple sec`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 2 dashes `Peychaud's Bitters`
- 10 cl `sparkling wine`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add Peychaud's Bitters
- Add bourbon
- Add triple sec
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add sparkling wine

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
