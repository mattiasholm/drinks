# Skeleton Key

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `elderflower liqueur`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 10 cl `ginger beer`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add bourbon
- Add elderflower liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add ginger beer
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass
