# Soundscape

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Aperol`
- 2 cl `orange juice`
- 1 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Aperol
- Add orange juice
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
