# Spanish Bomb

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `vodka`
- 2 cl `Licor 43`
- 8 cl `orange juice`
- 8 cl `milk`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add Licor 43
- Add orange juice
- Add milk
- Add ice and dry shake for 10 seconds, then shake for another 10 seconds

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- 5/5
