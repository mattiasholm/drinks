# Spring Feeling

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Chartreuse
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
