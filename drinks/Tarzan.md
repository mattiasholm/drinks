# Tarzan

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Campari`
- 1 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 5 cl `pineapple juice`
- 5 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add pineapple juice
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass
