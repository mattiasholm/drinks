# Ward Eight

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `rye whiskey`
- 2 cl `orange juice`
- 1 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add rye whiskey
- Add orange juice
- Add lemon juice
- Add grenadine
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
