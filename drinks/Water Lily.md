# Water Lily

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `violet liqueur`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add triple sec
- Add violet liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Nick & Nora glass
