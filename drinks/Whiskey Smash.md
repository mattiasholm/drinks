# Whiskey Smash

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 0.5 `lemon`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Muddle lemon
- Add bourbon
- Add simple syrup
- Add mint leaves
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
