# Airmail

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `honey syrup`
- 5 cl `sparkling wine`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add honey syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add sparkling wine

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
