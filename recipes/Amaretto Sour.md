# Amaretto Sour

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Disaronno`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- ½ `egg white`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Disaronno
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
