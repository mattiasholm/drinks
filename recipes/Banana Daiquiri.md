# Banana Daiquiri

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `Bols Banana`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add Bols Banana
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
