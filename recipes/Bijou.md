# Bijou

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Orange Bitters`
- 1 `maraschino cherry`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Orange Bitters
- Add gin
- Add Chartreuse
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add maraschino cherry

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
