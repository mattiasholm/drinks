# Blue Hawaii

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 4 cl `Bols Blue Curaçao`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 cl `pineapple juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add white rum
- Add Bols Blue Curaçao
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add pineapple juice
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice and stir

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass

## Rating:
- ★★★☆☆
