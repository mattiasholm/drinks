# Blue Hawaiian <!-- # Swimming Pool -->

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Malibu`
- 4 cl `Bols Blue Curaçao`
- 10 cl `pineapple juice`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Malibu
- Add Bols Blue Curaçao
- Add pineapple juice
- Add crushed ice and stir

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass
