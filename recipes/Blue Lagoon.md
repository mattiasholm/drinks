# Blue Lagoon

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `vodka`
- 2 cl `Bols Blue Curaçao`
- 1 cl `lime juice` <!-- - 1 cl `lemon juice` -->
- 10 cl `7 Up` <!-- - 12 cl `7 Up` -->
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add Bols Blue Curaçao
- Add lime juice
- Add crushed ice and stir
- Add 7 Up

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass

## Rating:
- ★★★☆☆
