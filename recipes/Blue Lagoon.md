# Blue Lagoon

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `vodka`
- 2 cl `Bols Blue Curaçao`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 10 cl `7 Up` <!-- - 12 cl `7 Up` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add Bols Blue Curaçao
- Add lime juice
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Fill up with 7 Up

## Served in:
- Highball glass

- ★★★☆☆
