# Bourbon Alexander

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `bourbon`
- 3 cl `Bols Cacao Brown`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add bourbon
- Add Bols Cacao Brown
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass
