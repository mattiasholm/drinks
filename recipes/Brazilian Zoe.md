# Brazilian Zoe

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `white rum`
- 1 cl `Campari`
- 1 cl `Bols Elderflower`
- 3 cl `grapefruit juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add white rum
- Add Campari
- Add Bols Elderflower
- Add grapefruit juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
