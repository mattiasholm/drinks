# Butterscotch Sour

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Bols Butterscotch`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup (1:1)`
- 0,5 `egg white`
- 2 dashes of `Angostura Bitter`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Bols Butterscotch
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Add 2 dashes of Angostura Bitter
- Dry shake for 10 seconds
- Add ice and shake for another 10 seconds

## Served in:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- 4/5
