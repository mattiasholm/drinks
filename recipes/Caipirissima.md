# Caipirissima

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 1 `lime`
- 2 tbsp `raw sugar`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Cut off the ends of the lime and slice it lengthwise
- Cut away the pith and slice each half in quarters
- Rub the glass with lime peel
- Add raw sugar
- Muddle the lime
- Add crushed ice and stir
- Add white rum

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★☆
