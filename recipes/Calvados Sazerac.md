# Calvados Sazerac

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `calvados`
- 1 cl `demerara syrup`
- 2 dashes `Peychaud's Bitters`
- 1 bar spoon `absinthe`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rinse the glass with absinthe
- Add Peychaud's Bitters
- Add calvados
- Add demerara syrup
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add lemon peel

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating
- ★★★★★
