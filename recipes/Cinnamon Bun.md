# Cinnamon Bun

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `Fireball`
- 2 cl `Licor 43`
- 8 cl `milk`
- 1 ml `cardamom`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Fireball
- Add Licor 43
- Add milk
- Add cardamom
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass
