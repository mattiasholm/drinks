# Clover Club

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `gin`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `raspberry syrup`
- ½ `egg white`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add lemon juice
- Add raspberry syrup
- Add egg white
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
