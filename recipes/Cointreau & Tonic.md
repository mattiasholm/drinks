# Cointreau & Tonic

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Cointreau`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 2 `lime wedges`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Rub lime peel around the glass
- Add Cointreau
- Add ice ball and stir
- Squeeze and add lime wedges
- Add tonic water

## Served in:
- Copa de Balon glass

- ★★★★☆
