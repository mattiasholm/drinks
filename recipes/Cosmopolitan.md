# Cosmopolitan

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `vodka`
- 2 cl `Cointreau`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 3 cl `cranberry juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add vodka
- Add Cointreau
- Add lime juice
- Add cranberry juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
