# Cuba Libre

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice` <!-- - 2-4 `lime wedges` -->
- 12 cl `Coca-Cola` <!-- 15 cl `Coca-Cola` - alla andra coladrinkar har 15 cl? Å andra sidan har 7Up-drinkarna bara 10-12 cl...-->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add white rum
- Add lime juice
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Fill up with Coca-Cola

## Served in:
- Highball glass
