# Cubata

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 12 cl `Coca-Cola`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add Coca-Cola
- Add ice cubes and stir

## Served in:
- Highball glass

- ★★★★☆
