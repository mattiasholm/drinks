# Daiquiri

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add white rum
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass
