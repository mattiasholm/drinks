# Dark & Stormy

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `dark rum`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 10 cl `ginger beer`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add ginger beer
- Add ice cubes

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★☆☆
