# Dark & Stormy

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `dark rum`
- 10 cl `ginger beer`
- 2 `lime wedges`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Rub lime peel around the glass
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add dark rum
- Squeeze and add lime wedges
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Add ginger beer

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★☆☆
