# Dirty Martini

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `dry vermouth`
- 1 bar spoon `olive brine`
- 3 `olives`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add dry vermouth
- Add olive brine
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add olives

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
