# Dirty Mojito

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 3 cl `demerara syrup`
- 8 cl `soda water`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add demerara syrup
- Muddle mint leaves
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add soda water
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
