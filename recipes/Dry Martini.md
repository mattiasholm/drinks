# Dry Martini

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `dry vermouth`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Rub lemon peel around the glass
- Add gin
- Add dry vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add lemon peel

## Served in:
- Martini glass
