# Dry Martini

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `dry vermouth`
- 3 `olives`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 2 minutes <!-- - Add ice cubes and stir for 1 minute -->
- Double strain
- Add rinsed olives

## Served in:
- Martini glass
