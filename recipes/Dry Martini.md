# Dry Martini

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `dry vermouth`
- 3 `olives` <!-- - 1 `lemon twist` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add dry vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 2 minutes <!-- - Add ice cubes and stir for 60 seconds -->
- Strain
- Add olives <!-- - Add lemon twist -->

## Served in:
- Martini glass
