# El Presidente

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 2 cl `dry vermouth`
- 1 cl `Cointreau`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add white rum
- Add dry vermouth
- Add Cointreau
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add lemon peel

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
