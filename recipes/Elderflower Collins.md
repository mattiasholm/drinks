# Elderflower Collins

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `genever`
- 2 cl `Bols Elderflower`
- 4 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup (1:1)`
- 10 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add genever
- Add Bols Elderflower
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice and stir
- Fill up with soda water
- Garnish with lemon slice

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
