# Enzoni

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `gin`
- 3 cl `Campari`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 5 `grapes`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Muddle grapes
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes and stir

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

- ★★★★☆
