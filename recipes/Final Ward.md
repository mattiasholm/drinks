# Final Ward

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `rye whiskey`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 cl `maraschino liqueur`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 `maraschino cherry`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add rye whiskey
- Add Chartreuse
- Add maraschino liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add maraschino cherry

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
