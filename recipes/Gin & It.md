# Gin & It

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Orange Bitters`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Orange Bitters
- Add gin
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
