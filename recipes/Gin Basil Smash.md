# Gin Basil Smash

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 `basil leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Rub the glass with basil leaves
- Add simple syrup
- Muddle the basil leaves
- Add lemon juice
- Add gin
- Add crushed ice and stir

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★☆☆
