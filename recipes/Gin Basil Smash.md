# Gin Basil Smash

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `gin` <!-- - 5 cl `gin` -->
- 2 cl `lemon juice` <!-- - 3 cl `lemon juice` -->
- 2 cl `simple syrup` <!-- - 2 cl `simple syrup` -->
- 10 `basil leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Muddle the basil leaves to release the flavors
- Add crushed ice and stir
- Add gin

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass
