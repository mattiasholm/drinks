# Gin Basil Smash

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- ½ `lemon`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 `basil leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Muddle lemon
- Add simple syrup
- Add gin
- Smack and add basil leaves
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

- ★★★★☆
