# Gin Fizz

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- ½ `egg white`
- 8 cl `soda water` <!-- - 5 cl `soda water` --> <!-- - 4 cl `soda water` --> <!-- - 3 cl `soda water` --> <!-- - 2 cl `soda water` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add soda water

## Glassware:
- Highball glass
