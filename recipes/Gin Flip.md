# Gin Flip

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 1 `egg`
- 1 pinch `nutmeg` <!--  -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add nutmeg <!--  -->

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
