# Gin Gin Mule

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 cl `ginger beer`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add simple syrup
- Muddle mint leaves
- Add gin
- Add lime juice
- Add ginger beer
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
