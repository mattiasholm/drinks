# Gin Old Fashioned

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `demerara syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions:
- Rub the glass with orange peel
- Add clear ice cube
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add demerara syrup
- Add gin
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add orange peel

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass
