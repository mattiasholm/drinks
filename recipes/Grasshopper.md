# Grasshopper

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `Bols Peppermint Green`
- 3 cl `Bols Cacao White`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Bols Peppermint Green
- Add Bols Cacao White
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
