# Hugo Spritz

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `Bols Elderflower`
- 9 cl `sparkling wine`
- 3 cl `soda water`
- 2 `lime wedges`
- 10 `mint leaves`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Bols Elderflower
- Muddle mint leaves
- Add lime wedges
- Add sparkling wine
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Copa glass

- ★★★★☆
