# Irish Coffee

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Irish whiskey`
- 2 tsp `brown sugar`
- 1,5 dl `hot coffee`
- 2 tbsp `whipped cream`

## Instructions:
- Add Irish whiskey
- Add brown sugar
- Fill up with hot coffee
- Add lightly whipped cream

## Served in:
- Coffee mug (preheated with hot water)

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
