# Irish Maid

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `Irish whiskey`
- 1 cl `Bols Elderflower`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 5 `cucumber slices`
- 10 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Muddle cucumber slices
- Add Irish whiskey
- Add Bols Elderflower
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add mint leaves
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
