# Kahlúa Sour

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Kahlúa`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 2 cl `egg white`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Kahlúa
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass
