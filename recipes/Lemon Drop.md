# Lemon Drop

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `vodka`
- 2 cl `Cointreau`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add Cointreau
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
