# Lemon Mojito

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 3 cl `demerara syrup`
- 8 cl `soda water`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add demerara syrup
- Muddle mint leaves
- Add white rum
- Add lemon juice
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
