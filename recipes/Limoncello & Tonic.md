# Limoncello & Tonic

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `limoncello`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 2 `lime wedges`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Rub the glass with lemon peel
- Add limoncello
- Squeeze and add lime wedges
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Add tonic water

## Served in:
- Copa de Balon glass
