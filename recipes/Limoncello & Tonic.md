# Limoncello & Tonic

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `limoncello`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 1 `lemon wedge`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Rub lemon peel around the glass
- Add limoncello
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Squeeze and add lemon wedge
- Add tonic water

## Served in:
- Copa de Balon glass

- ★★★★☆
