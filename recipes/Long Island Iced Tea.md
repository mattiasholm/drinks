# Long Island Iced Tea

## Ingredients:
- 1 cl `vodka`
- 1 cl `tequila`
- 1 cl `white rum`
- 1 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `Cointreau`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 cl `Coca-Cola`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add tequila
- Add white rum
- Add gin
- Add Cointreau
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add Coca-Cola
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Highball glass

- ★★★★☆
