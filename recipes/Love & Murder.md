# Love & Murder

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `Campari`
- 2 cl `Chartreuse`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 5 drops `saline solution`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Campari
- Add Chartreuse
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add saline solution
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
