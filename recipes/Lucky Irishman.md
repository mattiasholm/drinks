# Lucky Irishman

## Ingredients:
- 6 cl `Baileys`
- 2 cl `Irish whiskey`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Add Baileys
- Add Irish whiskey
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice ball

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★☆
