# Lucky Irishman

## Ingredients:
- 6 cl `Baileys` <!-- - 4 cl `Baileys` -->
- 2 cl `Irish whiskey` <!-- - 2 cl `Irish whiskey` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions:
- Add clear ice cube
- Add Baileys
- Add Irish whiskey
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass
