# Mai Tai

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `Cointreau`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `orgeat`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add dark rum
- Add Cointreau
- Add lime juice
- Add orgeat syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Highball glass
