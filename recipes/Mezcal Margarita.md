# Mezcal Margarita

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `mezcal`
- 2 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 pinch `salt`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rim the glass with salt
- Add mezcal
- Add triple sec
- Add lime juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
