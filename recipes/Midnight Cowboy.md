# Midnight Cowboy

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 1 cl `Southern Comfort`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 10 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add bourbon
- Add Southern Comfort
- Add lemon juice
- Add grenadine
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes and stir

## Served in:
- Highball glass
