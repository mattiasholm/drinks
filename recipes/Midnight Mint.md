# Midnight Mint

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Baileys`
- 2 cl `Minttu` <!-- - 2 cl `Bols Peppermint Green` -->
- 2 cl `cream`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Baileys
- Add Minttu <!-- - Add Bols Peppermint Green -->
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass
