# Milano Torino

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `Campari`
- 3 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions:
- Add clear ice cube
- Add Campari
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add orange peel

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass
