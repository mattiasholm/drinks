# Mint Julep

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `bourbon`
- 1 cl `raw sugar simple syrup`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add raw sugar simple syrup
- Muddle the mint leaves to release the flavors
- Add bourbon
- Add crushed ice and stir

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
