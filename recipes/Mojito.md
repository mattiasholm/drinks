# Mojito

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 3 cl `raw sugar simple syrup`
- 8 cl `soda water`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add raw sugar simple syrup
- Muddle the mint leaves to release the flavors
- Add lime juice
- Add white rum
- Add crushed ice and stir
- Add soda water


## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
