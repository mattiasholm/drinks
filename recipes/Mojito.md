# Mojito

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `white rum`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 3 cl `demerara syrup`
- 8 cl `soda water`
- 10 `mint leaves`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add demerara syrup
- Muddle mint leaves
- Add white rum
- Add lime juice
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass

## Rating
- ★★★★★
