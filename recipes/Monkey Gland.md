# Monkey Gland

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `gin`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 5 cl `orange juice`
- 1 bar spoon `absinthe`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rinse the glass with absinthe
- Add gin
- Add grenadine
- Add orange juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
