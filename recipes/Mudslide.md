# Mudslide

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Baileys`
- 2 cl `Kahlúa`
- 2 cl `vodka`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Baileys
- Add Kahlúa
- Add vodka
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
