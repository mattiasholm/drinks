# Negroni Flip

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Campari`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 1 `egg`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add Campari
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
