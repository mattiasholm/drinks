# New York Sour

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- ½ `egg white`
- 3 cl `red wine`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add bourbon
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes
- Add red wine

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating
- ★★★★★
