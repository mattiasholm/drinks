# Oaxacanite

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `tequila`
- 2 cl `mezcal`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `honey syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add tequila
- Add mezcal
- Add lime juice
- Add honey syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
