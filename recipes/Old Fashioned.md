# Old Fashioned

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `bourbon`
- 1 cl `raw sugar simple syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Bitter`
- 1 `orange twist`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Add bourbon
- Add raw sugar simple syrup
- Add Angostura Bitter
- Add ice cubes and stir gently for 2 minutes
- Double strain
- Add ice ball
- Add orange twist

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
