# Painkiller

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 tbsp `coconut cream`
- 3 cl `simple syrup`
- 2 cl `orange juice`
- 8 cl `pineapple juice`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add dark rum
- Add coconut cream
- Add simple syrup
- Add orange juice
- Add pineapple juice
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice and stir

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
