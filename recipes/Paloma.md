# Paloma

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `tequila`
- 1 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `agave syrup`
- 5 cl `grapefruit juice`
- 5 cl `soda water`
- 1 ml `salt`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Rim the glass with salt
- Add tequila
- Add lime juice
- Add agave syrup
- Add grapefruit juice
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass

## Rating
- ★★★★☆
