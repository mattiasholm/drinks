# Passion Fruit Punch

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 cl `passion fruit juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Add passion fruit juice

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass
