# Pegu Club

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add gin
- Add triple sec
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
