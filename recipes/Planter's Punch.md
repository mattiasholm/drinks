# Planter's Punch

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 5 cl `orange juice`
- 5 cl `pineapple juice`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add dark rum
- Add lime juice
- Add grenadine
- Add simple syrup
- Add orange juice
- Add pineapple juice
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice and stir

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass
