# Queen's Park Swizzle

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `dark rum`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `demerara syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add demerara syrup
- Smack and muddle mint leaves
- Add lime juice
- Add dark rum
- Add crushed ice

## Served in:
- Highball glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
