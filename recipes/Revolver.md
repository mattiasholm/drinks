# Revolver

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `coffee liqueur`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Orange Bitters`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Orange Bitters
- Add bourbon
- Add coffee liqueur
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add orange peel

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
