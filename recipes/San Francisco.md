# San Francisco

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `vodka`
- 2 cl `Bols Banana`
- 2 cl `grenadine` <!-- - 1 cl `grenadine` -->
- 15 cl `orange juice` <!-- - 12 cl `orange juice` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add Bols Banana
- Add orange juice
- Add ice cubes and stir
- Add grenadine

## Served in:
- Highball glass
