# Santiago

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `white rum`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 5 cl `orange juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add white rum
- Add grenadine
- Add orange juice
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass
