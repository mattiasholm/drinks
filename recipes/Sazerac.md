# Sazerac

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `rye whiskey`
- 1 cl `demerara syrup`
- 2 dashes `Peychaud's Bitters`
- 1 bar spoon `absinthe`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Rinse the glass with absinthe
- Add Peychaud's Bitters
- Add demerara syrup
- Add rye whiskey
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add lemon peel

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
