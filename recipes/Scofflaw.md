# Scofflaw

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `rye whiskey`
- 2 cl `dry vermouth`
- 1 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `grenadine`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Orange Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Orange Bitters
- Add rye whiskey
- Add dry vermouth
- Add lemon juice
- Add grenadine
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
