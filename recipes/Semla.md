# Semla

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Amaretto`
- 6 cl `milk`
- 1 ml `cardamom`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Amaretto
- Add milk
- Add cardamom
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain
- Garnish with cardamom

## Served in:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
