# Sex On The Beach

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `Peachtree`
- 3 cl `vodka`
- 8 cl `orange juice`
- 8 cl `cranberry juice`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Peachtree
- Add vodka
- Add crushed ice and stir
- Add orange juice
- Add cranberry juice

## Served in:
- Hurricane glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
