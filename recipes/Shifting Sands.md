# Shifting Sands

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `maraschino liqueur`
- 1 cl `lemon juice`
- 5 cl `grapefruit juice`
- 5 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add gin
- Add maraschino liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add grapefruit juice
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Highball glass
