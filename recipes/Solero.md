# Solero

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Licor 43` <!-- - 4 cl `Licor 43` -->
- 6 cl `passion fruit juice`
- 8 cl `milk`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Licor 43
- Add passion fruit juice
- Add milk
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass
