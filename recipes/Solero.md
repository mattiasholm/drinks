# Solero

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Licor 43`
- 8 cl `passion fruit juice` <!-- - 6 cl `passion fruit juice` -->
- 8 cl `milk` <!-- - 6 cl `milk` -->
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Licor 43
- Add passion fruit juice
- Add milk
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Highball glass
