# Spanish Bomb

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Licor 43`
- 8 cl `orange juice`
- 8 cl `milk`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Licor 43
- Add orange juice
- Add milk
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Served in:
- Highball glass

- ★★★★★
