# Spanish Bomb

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `Licor 43` <!-- - 4 cl `Licor 43` -->
- 2 cl `vodka` <!-- - 0 cl `vodka` -->
- 8 cl `orange juice`
- 8 cl `milk`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Licor 43
- Add vodka
- Add orange juice
- Add milk
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes and stir

## Served in:
- Highball glass
