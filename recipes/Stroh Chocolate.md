# Stroh Chocolate

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `Stroh 80`
- 1 tbsp `sugar`
- 1 tbsp `cacao powder`
- 20 cl `milk`
- 3 cl `cream`

## Instructions:
- Heat up the milk, sugar and cacao powder
- Add Stroh 80
- Add hot chocolate
- Add lightly whipped cream
- Garnish with mini marshmallows

## Served in:
- Tea mug

- ★★★★★
