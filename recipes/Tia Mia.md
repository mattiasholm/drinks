# Tia Mia

## Ingredients
- 2 cl `mezcal`
- 2 cl `dark rum`
- 1 cl `triple sec`
- 2 cl `lime juice`
-  cl `orgeat`
- 24 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add mezcal
- Add dark rum
- Add triple sec
- Add lime juice
- Add orgeat
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add crushed ice

## Glassware
- Highball glass
