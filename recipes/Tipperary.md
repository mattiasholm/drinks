# Tipperary

## Ingredients
- 4 cl `Irish whiskey`
- 2 cl `sweet vermouth`
- 1 cl `Chartreuse`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add Irish whiskey
- Add sweet vermouth
- Add Chartreuse
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add orange peel

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
