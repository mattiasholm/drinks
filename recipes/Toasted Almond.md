# Toasted Almond

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `coffee liqueur`
- 2 cl `amaretto`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add coffee liqueur
- Add amaretto
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass
