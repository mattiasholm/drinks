# Toasted Almond

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `coffee liqueur`
- 2 cl `Disaronno`
- 4 cl `cream`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add coffee liqueur
- Add Disaronno
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
