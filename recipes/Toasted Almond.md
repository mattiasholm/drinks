# Toasted Almond

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `Kahlúa`
- 2 cl `Disaronno`
- 4 cl `cream`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions:
- Add clear ice cube
- Add Kahlúa
- Add Disaronno
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
