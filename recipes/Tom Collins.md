# Tom Collins

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `gin`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup (1:1)`
- 10 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add ice and stir
- Fill up with soda water
- Garnish with lemon slice

## Served in:
- Copa glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
