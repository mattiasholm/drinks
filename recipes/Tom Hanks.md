# Tom Hanks

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Peachtree` <!-- - 5 cl `Peachtree` -->
- 2 cl `lime juice`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 20 `cilantro leaves`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add lime juice
- Muddle cilantro leaves
- Add Peachtree
- Add tonic water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Copa de Balon glass
