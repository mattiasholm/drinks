# Tom Hanks

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Peachtree`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 20 `cilantro leaves`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add lime juice
- Smack and muddle cilantro leaves
- Add Peachtree
- Add tonic water
- Add ice cubes and stir

## Served in:
- Copa de Balon glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
