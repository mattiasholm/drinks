# Tom Hanks

## Ingredients:
- 6 cl `Peachtree`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 20 `cilantro leaves`
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add lime juice
- Muddle the cilantro leaves to release the flavors
- Add Peachtree
- Add crushed ice and stir
- Fill up with tonic water

## Served in:
- Copa glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
