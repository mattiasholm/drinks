# Tomate

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `Pernod`
- 1 cl `grenadine` <!-- - 2 cl `grenadine` -->
- 2 cl `water` <!-- - 3 cl `water` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Add Pernod
- Add grenadine
- Add water
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add ice ball

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass
