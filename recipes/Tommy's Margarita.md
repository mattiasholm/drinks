# Tommy's Margarita

## Ingredients
- 5 cl `tequila`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `agave syrup`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add tequila
- Add lime juice
- Add agave syrup
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating
- ★★★★★
