# Trinidad Sour

## Ingredients
- 3 cl `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 2 cl `rye whiskey`
- 3 cl `orgeat`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add rye whiskey
- Add orgeat
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware
- Coupe glass
