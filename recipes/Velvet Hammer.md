# Velvet Hammer

## Ingredients:
- 3 cl `Cointreau`
- 3 cl `coffee liqueur`
- 3 cl `cream`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Cointreau
- Add coffee liqueur
- Add cream
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
