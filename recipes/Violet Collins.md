# Violet Collins

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `violet liqueur`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup`
- 10 cl `soda water`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add violet liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add soda water
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Highball glass

- ★★★☆☆
