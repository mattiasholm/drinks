# Vodka Gimlet

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `vodka`
- 3 cl `lime juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add lime juice
- Add simple syrup
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
