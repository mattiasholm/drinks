# Vodka Old Fashioned

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `vodka`
- 1 cl `demerara syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 `lemon peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `clear ice cube`

## Instructions:
- Add clear ice cube
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add demerara syrup
- Add vodka
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Squeeze and add lemon peel

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass
