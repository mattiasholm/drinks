# Vodka Old Fashioned

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `vodka`
- 1 cl `raw sugar simple syrup`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 1 `orange peel`
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add raw sugar simple syrup
- Add vodka
- Add ice cubes and stir for 30 seconds
- Strain
- Add ice ball
- Add orange peel

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass

## Rating:
- ★★★☆☆
