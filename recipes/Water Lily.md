# Water Lily

## Ingredients:
- 2 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Cointreau`
- 2 cl `violet liqueur`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add Cointreau
- Add violet liqueur
- Add lemon juice
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
