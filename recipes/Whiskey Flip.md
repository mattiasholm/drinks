# Whiskey Flip

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 1 `egg`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters
- Add bourbon
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Coupe glass

## Rating:
- ★★★★★
