# Whiskey Flip

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 2 cl `simple syrup` <!-- - 1 cl `simple syrup` -->
- 1 `egg`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Aromatic Bitters` <!--  -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add Angostura Aromatic Bitters <!--  -->
- Add simple syrup
- Add bourbon
- Add egg
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
