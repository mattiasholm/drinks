# Whiskey Smash

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- ½ `lemon`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 15 `mint leaves`
- 16 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Muddle lemon
- Add simple syrup
- Add bourbon
- Add mint leaves
- Dry shake for 15 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain
- Add ice cubes

## Glassware:
- Old Fashioned glass
