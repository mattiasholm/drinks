# Whiskey Sour

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `bourbon`
- 3 cl `lemon juice`
- 2 cl `simple syrup`
- 2 cl `egg white`
- 2 dashes `Angostura Bitter`
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add bourbon
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup
- Add egg white
- Add Angostura Bitter
- Dry shake for 30 seconds
- Add ice cubes and shake for 10 seconds
- Double strain

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass
