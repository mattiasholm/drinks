# Whiskey Tonic

## Ingredients:
- 5 cl `Irish whiskey` <!-- - 5 cl `bourbon` -->
- 10 cl `tonic water`
- 2 `lime wedges`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Rub lime peel around the glass
- Add Irish whiskey <!-- - Add bourbon -->
- Add ice ball and stir
- Squeeze and add lime wedges
- Add tonic water

## Served in:
- Copa de Balon glass
