# White Lady

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `gin`
- 2 cl `Cointreau`
- 2 cl `lemon juice`
- 1 cl `simple syrup` <!-- -->
- ½ `egg white` <!-- -->
- 8 `ice cubes`

## Instructions:
- Add gin
- Add Cointreau
- Add lemon juice
- Add simple syrup <!-- -->
- Add egg white <!-- -->
- Dry shake for 15 seconds <!-- -->
- Add ice cubes and shake for 15 seconds
- Double strain

## Glassware:
- Coupe glass
