# White Russian

## Ingredients:
- 4 cl `vodka` <!-- - 2 cl `vodka` -->
- 2 cl `Kahlúa`
- 2 cl `cream` <!-- - 4 cl `cream` -->
- 8 `ice cubes`
- 1 `ice ball`

## Instructions:
- Add vodka
- Add Kahlúa
- Add ice cubes and stir gently for 2 minutes <!-- - Add ice cubes and stir -->
- Double strain
- Add cream
- Add ice ball

## Served in:
- Old Fashioned glass
